*****  Cloudflare settings that may need adjustment

After going through both Nextcloud and Cloudflare I have found that Disabling Rocket Loader™ from Cloudflare fix the problem.
How to Fix:

    Login to cloudflare
    Navigate to Speed Under your site.
    Under Optimization Locate Rocket Loader™
    Turn it off
    Reload or Refresh your site.
    Done.
